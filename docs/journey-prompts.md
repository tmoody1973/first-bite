# First Bite — Journey Prompts

Prompts inspired by the spirit of food-focused travel storytelling. The kind of places where the real food lives — not restaurants with PR teams, but stalls, carts, kitchens, and markets where the cooking hasn't changed in generations.

## Best for Demo

- **"Street food in Lagos"** — vibrant, bold, strong visuals
- **"Night markets of Bangkok"** — iconic, guaranteed strong imagery
- **"Tacos in Oaxaca"** — rich history, mole, indigenous cuisine
- **"Grandmother kitchens of Tbilisi"** — Georgian food, nobody talks about it
