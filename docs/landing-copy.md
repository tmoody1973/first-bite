# First Bite — Landing Page Copy

## Headline
First Bite

## Tagline
The real story starts where the guidebook ends.

## Subheadline
Name a place and we'll take you on a 5-stop food journey — AI-generated photography, real restaurant recommendations, home-cookable recipes, narrated storytelling, ambient soundscapes, and a vintage travel poster. All from one prompt.

## Prompt Input Placeholder
Name a place. I'll find the food.

## Suggestion Pills
- Night markets of Bangkok
- Tacos in Oaxaca
- Street food in Lagos
- Ramen alleys of Tokyo
- Hawker centres of Singapore
- Bistros of Lyon

## What Happens When You Hit Go

### The loading screen (while your journey generates)
- Stop 1 — The Arrival: first impression, chaos, the smell
- Stop 2 — The Street: the stall nobody talks about
- Stop 3 — The Kitchen: behind the counter, the technique
- Stop 4 — The Table: strangers sharing food and stories
- Stop 5 — The Last Bite: what this place taught you

### Each stop includes
- **AI-generated scene photography** — street markets, kitchens, dining tables
- **AI-generated dish photography** — the signature plate at each stop
- **Real restaurant data** — verified through Google Places with ratings and real photos
- **Street View imagery** — see the actual location from street level
- **Home-cookable recipe** — full ingredients and step-by-step instructions, downloadable as PNG
- **Narrated storytelling** — a distinctive voice reads the story of each stop
- **Ambient soundscapes** — market bustle, sizzling woks, clinking glasses, quiet footsteps
- **Map integration** — tap any place to view on Google Maps

### The finale
- **Vintage travel poster** — a French lithograph-style poster featuring all five dishes and restaurants from your journey
- **Cinematic summary video** — an 8-second Veo-generated travel reel of the journey
- **Download poster** — save it as your keepsake
- **Share link** — send the full journey to anyone

### Your dashboard
- Every journey is saved to your gallery
- A world map tracks all the places you've explored
- Revisit any journey and experience it again with full narration and sound

## Footer / Disclaimer
All places and restaurants are AI-suggested. Verify details before visiting.

## Feature breakdown (for marketing / social)

**8 modalities from one prompt:**
1. Narrative text
2. AI-generated scene images
3. AI-generated dish images
4. Real restaurant photos (Google Places)
5. Street View imagery
6. Ambient soundscapes (ElevenLabs)
7. Voice narration (Gemini TTS)
8. Cinematic video (Veo 3.1)

Plus: vintage travel poster, downloadable recipe cards, world map dashboard, shareable journeys.

**Powered by:**
- Gemini 3.1 Flash Image (Nano Banana 2) — interleaved text + image
- Gemini 3.1 Pro — orchestrator
- Gemini 2.5 Flash TTS — narration
- Veo 3.1 — video generation
- Google Places API — restaurant verification
- Street View Static API — location imagery
- Google Search Grounding — fact verification
- ElevenLabs — ambient sound effects
- Google Cloud Run + Firestore + Cloud Storage
